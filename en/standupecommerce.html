Stand Up eCommerce

controplan.com.br/​standupecommerce/

The Stand Up eCommerce was a event featured by ControPlan with HG e-commerce held in Teresópolis, Brazil in November 2015.

The purpose of it is to promote the e-commerce between the small and medium-sized retailers of the region with lectures and talks on the subject.
