PDVend Whitelabel for Mercado Livre

SaaS rebranding

Nexaas is a company of digital solutions for business with a wide range of financial softwares. Their vision is that monolytics ERP sytems are a thing of the past and that micro-services is the new way to go.

In 2018 they were presented with a inevitable shift from targeting small business to medium and big. That shift required a major overhaul at their image, both external and internal, and a bigger chalenge: develop a systematic identity for its fragmented portfolio and communication.
